Music

I make electronic music under the moniker Trope. You can find my music on SoundCloud, Spotify, Apple Music, and Bandcamp.

I primarily use FL Studio, although I have used Ableton Live quite a bit, too. I like to play around with samples, often creating instruments out of samples I found on the Internet or recorded myself.

I make music as a hobby, and I’m constantly learning! You can keep up with my music here.
